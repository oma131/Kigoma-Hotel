Rooms & Suites

A holistic approach to luxury living, the Kigoma offers 297 curated rooms & suites that ensure that guests would never want, or need, to leave. Guests are invited to customize their luxury stay with a selection of the most exquisite room offerings available, including seven distinct suite categories featuring private and open floor layouts as well as outdoor terraces and city and garden-view balcony options.

Popular Offerings

Garden Premier

Pleasant room, classic style king size bed, carpeted, wooden or tiled floors with a choice of pool or city view

Family Suite

Spacious bedroom with king size bed and adjoining small bedroom with small working area, a choice of a single bed or two and a restroom. Perfect for family.

Facilities

Meeting Room

A 20-seater meeting room is on ground at the Kigamo for your business meetings and client negotiations.

Restaurant

Upscale fine dining restaurant and bar serving a variety of local, continental and international dishes.

Swimming Pool

An Olympic size pool, two standard size pools and a kiddies' pool, each offering views of the game reserve.

Conference Hall

Two 150-seater conference halls equipped with smart monitors for presentations for conferences and meet-ups.

Movie Theater

Three 50-seater movie rooms with standard screens are available to visitors at Kigamo.

Lounge

Open lounge with a view of the game reserve and a bar for refreshments. The lounge also features a live band.
